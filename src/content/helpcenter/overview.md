## What this is

A marketplace for **managed funds on Polymarket**. Fund managers publish a thesis and raise a pool. Investors **commit capital** (a mandate). When the manager trades, those trades fan out across every active mandate in proportion to size.

You connect a wallet (Privy), register a **Polymarket deposit wallet** on Polygon, and fund it with **pUSD**. Commitments lock deposit-wallet balance until you withdraw unused cash during the raise or the fund closes.

## Roles

- **Manager** — create a fund, raise capital, place trades, close the fund.
- **Investor** — browse funds, commit during the raise, follow positions and PnL, receive settlement on close.
