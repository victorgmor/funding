## Mandates

A **mandate** is your commitment to a fund: how much notional you’ve locked and how much cash is still deployable for trades.

- Minimum commit: **$5**
- Backed by liquid **pUSD** in your Polymarket deposit wallet
- Open mandate notional is locked — it doesn’t count as withdrawable deposit balance

Managers don’t see the investor commit UI on their own funds.

## During the raise

While the fund is in the **deposit** stage you can:

- **Add capital** to an existing mandate (or open one)
- **Withdraw unused deployable cash** back to your deposit wallet

Capital already tied up in positions stays in the fund until close.

## After the raise

New commits stop. Your mandate rides through trading and settles when the fund closes — see [Settlement](/docs/settlement).
