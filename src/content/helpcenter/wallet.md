## Connect

Use **Log in** in the nav to connect with Privy. The app runs on **Polygon**.

## Polymarket deposit wallet

Commitments use a dedicated **Polymarket deposit wallet**, not your main Privy wallet balance alone.

1. Connect your wallet  
2. Register / create the deposit wallet (Privy approval)  
3. Move **pUSD** into the deposit wallet  
4. Commit to funds from that balance  

In the account menu you’ll see deposit address, balance, committed (locked), and **withdrawable** amounts.

## Withdrawable

Withdrawable deposit ≈ deposit balance − capital locked in open mandates. Fund a mandate only with liquid deposit pUSD.
